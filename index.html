Fundamental particles

Protons, neutrons and electrons as you probably know make up matter. But did you know that protons and neutrons belong to HADRONS, wich is what physicist call particles that are made out of QUARKS ?

and that electrons are just one type of LEPTON?

protons

  • - positive charge
  • - made of quarks
  • - part of the HADRONS family

neutrons

  • - neutral charge
  • - made of quarks
  • - part of the HADRONS family

electrons

  • - negative charge
  • - elementary particles
  • - part of the LEPTONS family
